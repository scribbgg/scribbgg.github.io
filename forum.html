Discussion Forum

Share your experiences, report bugs, and discuss the collection

Welcome to the Small Things Matter discussion forum!

This is a place to share your thoughts, report issues, request features, and help fellow users. Feel free to start a new discussion or join an existing one.

When reporting bugs, please include:

  • Detailed description - What happened? What did you expect? Did you install other modifications? What are your system specs?
  • Steps to reproduce - How can I recreate the issue?
  • Crash logs - Share logs if the game crashed (Documents → My Games → Skyrim Special Edition → SKSE). To do this, consider using Pastebin and attach the link to your file.
  • Screenshots - Visual evidence is very helpful sometimes! To attach them, consider using Imgur.

Note: You'll need a GitHub account to participate. It's free and takes just a couple of minutes to create.
